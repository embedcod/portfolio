Our Core Technology

Intelligent. Configurable. Connected.

SafiCell isn't just a circuit board; it's an operating system for batteries. Our proprietary hardware and firmware bridge the gap between varied battery chemistries and reliable home power.

Multi-Protocol Connectivity

CAN, RS485, I²C, BLE, and IoT. Works with any inverter.

Cloud & OTA Updates

Remote diagnostics and firmware upgrades to improve efficiency over time.

Dual-Input Active Balancing

Intelligently manages Solar vs Grid charging to maximize cell life.
